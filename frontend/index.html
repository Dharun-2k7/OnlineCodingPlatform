Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of student developers and competitive programmers from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Nagercoil Campus, passionate about logic, algorithms, and pushing our coding limits.

This platform was developed by students, for students, to host our weekly Reverse Coding and Code Golf contests. We believe every challenge is an opportunity to sharpen our skills and connect with like-minded problem solvers.
